Rules

1. Students are not allowed to enter the Forbidden Forest.

2. Fighting is not tolerated at Hogwarts. Detentions will be handed out and points will be docked.

3. Students are to remain in their dormitories or common rooms after ten at night. -only 5th year and above are allowed out on the corridors until nine o'clock

4. No magic is to be done in the corridors between classes.(you can do this)

5. First Year students are not allowed a broom.

6. The only pets allowed are small pets, such as an owl, a cat, a rat or a toad.

7. In order to get to the restricted section, you must have a signed note by a professor.

8. Students are not allowed to use magic over the school holidays.

9. Only Third Year students and above are allowed to Hogsmeade.

10. Love potions are not allowed at Hogwarts.

11. No food or drinks inside the classes or the library.

12. Cheating is not tolerated at Hogwarts.

13. Be respectful of the Hogwarts Staff.

14. Do not rp other people's charries without their consent.

15. Only teachers that are deatheaters can use Unforgivables.

16. Do not kill another person's charrie unless they're ok with it(in the forbidden forest or fighting a deatheater)

17. You may cuss but please keep it to a minimum.

18. Do not argue with or disobey mods
